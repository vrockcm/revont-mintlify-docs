This guide walks you through everything you need to go from a fresh Revont account to a fully configured AI-assisted call. By the end, Revont will be connected to your calendar and video conferencing tool, ready to join your next meeting and surface live guidance the moment it starts.

<Note>
  **Prerequisites**

  Before you begin, make sure you have the following:
  - A Revont account — sign up for free at [app.revont.ai](https://app.revont.ai)
  - Admin access to your Revont workspace if you're setting Revont up for a team
  - A supported video conferencing tool: **Zoom**, **Google Meet**, or **Microsoft Teams**
</Note>

## Setup steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in to Revont">
    Go to [app.revont.ai](https://app.revont.ai) and sign in with Google or your company SSO. If this is your first time signing in, Revont prompts you to create a workspace — give it your company name and choose whether you're setting it up for yourself or your team. Your workspace is the central hub where all your calls, team members, playbooks, and settings live.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your calendar">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations → Calendar** and click **Connect** next to Google Calendar or Microsoft Outlook. Authorize the permissions when prompted. Once connected, Revont scans your upcoming meetings and automatically detects calls that match your video conferencing setup. You'll see those meetings appear in the **Calls** dashboard as scheduled sessions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect your video conferencing tool">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations → Video Conferencing** and connect Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams. Revont joins your calls as a silent AI participant — your prospects and other attendees won't be prompted to interact with it or install anything. Once this integration is active, Revont will automatically join any calendar meeting that includes a supported video conferencing link.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Join a call">
    Start or join a scheduled meeting as you normally would. Revont's assistant panel appears on-screen alongside your video conferencing window, displaying live guidance as the conversation unfolds. You'll see relevant talk tracks, objection responses, and battle cards surface in real time — no manual input needed. Your prospect and other attendees see nothing on their end.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review your summary">
    After the call ends, open the **Calls** tab in the sidebar. Your AI-generated summary is ready within 2 minutes and includes a structured recap of the conversation, a list of extracted action items, a full transcript with speaker labels, and a coaching score with highlights. From here you can edit the summary, reassign action items, or push everything directly to your CRM.
  </Step>
</Steps>
