<Update label="v1.0.15-beta" description="2026-07-05">

First-class Contacts, a unified Reporting & Forecasting workspace, and faster knowledge-base imports.

## New
- Org-wide Contacts page with detail, timeline, and contact-client relationships
- Unified Analytics & Reports with forecasting, quotas, and win-loss
- Ask analytics questions in chat and get charts inline
- Bulk-import knowledge base files by drag-and-drop

## Improved
- Slack-style labeled side navigation with a More overflow
- Faster, more reliable knowledge-base PDF conversion

## Fixed
- CRM-imported records keep their original created dates
- Various reporting, chat, and knowledge-base fixes

<Update label="v1.0.14-beta" description="2026-06-28">

Real-time two-way CRM sync with Salesforce and HubSpot, plus configurable field mapping and custom fields.

## New
- Two-way sync with Salesforce and HubSpot - changes flow both directions in real time
- Configurable field mapping with a single active CRM provider
- Custom fields for clients, deals, and contacts, with the option to import them straight from your CRM
- A review queue to vet incoming CRM records before they're applied

## Improved
- Clearer field mapping: origin badges, brand chips, auto-matched options, and properly capitalized labels
- Faster background backfill that scales to 100k+ records

## Fixed
- Sign-in sessions are now correctly scoped to your organization

</Update>
