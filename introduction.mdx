Revont sits alongside you on every sales call — listening, analyzing, and acting — so you can stay focused on the conversation instead of scrambling for the right response or worrying about what to log afterward. For sales teams, every call is a chance to win or lose a deal. Revont makes sure you walk into each one prepared, stay sharp throughout, and leave nothing behind when it ends.

## What Revont does

### 1. Live call guidance

As your conversation unfolds, Revont's AI surfaces the right information at the right moment — relevant talk tracks, objection responses, and battle cards that match exactly what's being discussed. You don't need to search for anything or break your flow. If a prospect raises a concern about pricing, Revont surfaces your pricing objection playbook. If a competitor comes up, the relevant battle card appears automatically.

### 2. Automatic summaries

When the call ends, Revont gets to work. Within minutes, you receive a structured recap that covers the key moments of the conversation, objections raised by the prospect, commitments made by both sides, and suggested next steps to move the deal forward. Every rep on your team walks away with the same quality of notes — regardless of experience level.

### 3. CRM sync

Revont pushes action items, notes, and call summaries directly to the contact record in your CRM. Reps never need to manually log a call again. Updates land in the right place automatically, so your pipeline data stays accurate and your team spends time selling instead of doing data entry.

---

## Who it's for

**Sales reps** use Revont as a real-time co-pilot — getting the guidance they need in the moment and never missing a follow-up after the call.

**Sales managers and coaches** use Revont to review call recordings and AI-generated coaching scores, identify where reps need support, and ensure playbooks are being followed consistently across the team.

**Revenue operations teams** use Revont to enforce CRM hygiene, build and maintain playbooks, configure integrations, and get a clean data pipeline from every customer conversation.

## How to get started

Getting up and running with Revont takes less than 15 minutes:

- **Create your account and workspace** — Sign up at [app.revont.ai](https://app.revont.ai) and set up your team workspace. See the [Quickstart guide](/quickstart) for a full walkthrough.
- **Connect your video conferencing tool** — Authorize Zoom, Google Meet, or Microsoft Teams so Revont can join your calls automatically. Visit [Video Conferencing Integrations](/integrations/video-conferencing) for setup instructions.
- **Connect your CRM** — Link Salesforce or HubSpot so call data flows directly into your contact records. Visit [CRM Integrations](/integrations/crm) to get connected.
